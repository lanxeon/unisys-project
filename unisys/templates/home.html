How does our product work?

Sign In First!

Let's say Rahul wants to use our product, he can sign in using the sign in button on the home page, if he's a new user, he/she can register first.

Start Chatting!

Once Signed in, Rahul can start chatting with any other registered user. If Rahul wants to chat with Neha, he can just click on the chat button and start right away!

Communicate using your own language.

We have made sure that differently abled people like Rahul can also use our product just as any other user. He/She can perform the words they want to convey as gestures onto their webcam and see the magic!

How does the magic happen?

The gestures as performed by Rahul are converted into text and audio using gTTS and an intelligent Deep Learning model! Simple right?

How about on the other end?

Neha, who's on the other end of the chat, can raed the text or listen to the words gestured by Rahul and can chat the same way. That's it!
